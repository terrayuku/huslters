We organise ourselves so that we are where our customers are, this includes selling at events and participate in pop-up market events. We are currently based in Pretoria but we render our service across the country since we deliver everywhere.

    H.L.D has one thing clear in mind: Customer results. From concept to execution the team works with clients to achieve the best possible results to deliver on our promise.
